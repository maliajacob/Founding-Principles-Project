THREE GREAT MINDS

America's unique government unprecedentedly gave power to the people. Federalism serves as the keystone, enabling all 50 states to coexist harmoniously even through hard times. Other countries have drafted similar government documents in the likeness of the U.S. Constitution. They recognize its capacity for developing a strong society. While you may have heard of the Founding Fathers, did you know that their ideas were not entirely original? When the Constitution was crafted, its authors drew heavily from the ideas of Enlightenment (Age of Reason) philosophers. Through them, the Founding Fathers were inspired to create a government that ensures justice, safety, and appropriate powers.
